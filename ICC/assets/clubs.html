CLUBS

This are the 15 clubs of IIT Bombay which offers different cultural activities and are passionate for their cult.

Fourthwall

Dramatics Clubs of IIT Bombay

The Fourthwall Club at IIT Bombay is a vibrant club focused on promoting theater and performing arts within the institute. It provides students a platform to explore their interest in acting, direction, scriptwriting, production, and various aspects of theater. The club is dedicated to nurturing creative expression, whether in drama, street plays, or full-fledged theater productions.
